About Us

Inner Healing Therapy, A Licensed Clinical Social Worker P.C.

There is a substantial difference between the rates at which men and women seek therapy. There are many misconceptions around men seeking therapy, which have played a role in these differences. One of the most significant mistaken beliefs is that men should be strong and stoic, and that showing vulnerability or seeking help is a sign of weakness. These stereotypes discourage some men from seeking help when they need it. The stigma surrounding vulnerability and emotional expression can lead men to minimize their mental health needs or try to cope in silence. This doesn’t have to be the case. The reality is that seeking help is a sign of strength and courage. Therapy is the Ultimate Mental Gym!
